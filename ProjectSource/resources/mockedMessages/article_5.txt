Your  searches will cover every nook and cranny, too, including titles,  subjects, body content, names, identities, tags, priorities,  statuses, attachments – even custom fields.</p>  <p>Although you can get going using the search bar right out of  the gate, there&#39;s a lot you can do with it that might not be  immediately obvious. Using    <code>&lt;</code> would limit it to conversations older than    the date.</em></li>    <li><strong>Look for what&#39;s missing.</strong> Kayako can also    filter your results according to whether a particular field is    empty by using null as your search value.<br>    <em>Example: Searching for <code>&#34;replacement parts&#34;    -percolators in:conversations created&gt;2016-01-01    assignee:null</code> will give you only those recent    non-percolator parts conversations that have not yet been    assigned.</em></li>  </ul>  <p>The examples above are just the tip of the iceberg.
